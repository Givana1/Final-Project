Maintenance & Restoration

Keeping your Mitsubishi 3000GT in top condition requires regular maintenance and occasional restoration.

Here are some tips and guides to help you maintain and restore your 3000GT.

Maintenance Tips

  • Regular oil changes
  • Check and replace air filters
  • Inspect and maintain the suspension system
  • Keep the tires properly inflated
